About the hair

At the beginning of the series, Zuko has a distinctive tail known as a 'sikha.' The sikha represents the spiritual connection and obedience of firebenders to the Fire Lord and their national identity. Zuko's sikha is an important symbol of his loyalty to the Fire Nation and his desire to find the Avatar, Aang, in order to restore honor to his family and himself.

About the outfit

During his exile in the Earth Kingdom city of Ba Sing Se, Zuko wears a simpler and more casual outfit, without his topknot. This attire represents his temporary separation from the Fire Nation and his quest for a new life.

The techniques he has adopted from the nation.

episode 16,19 and 21 season 3

Zuko learned the technique "Generating Lightning" from his uncle Iroh, a master of firebending and lightning control. This advanced technique requires not only powerful firebending skills but also an understanding of inner balance and control over chi. Zuko was one of the few who successfully mastered this technique. It later became a significant weapon in his arsenal and played a crucial role in the series.
